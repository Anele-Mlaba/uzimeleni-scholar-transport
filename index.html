Who We Are

Zimeleni Scholar Transport Association was founded in 2018 by a group of dedicated transport operators who recognised the need for a structured, accountable, and safe scholar transport service in the KwaZulu-Natal community.

The name "Uzimeleni" means "stand up for yourself" — reflecting our commitment to empowering transport operators while upholding the highest standards of safety and service for learners.

Today we operate across multiple schools in the greater Durban region, transporting hundreds of learners daily under a strict code of conduct that every member is obliged to uphold.

Safety First

Vehicles regularly inspected. Drivers vetted and professionally trained.

Community Driven

Run by local operators who understand the community's transport needs.

Accountable

Transparent governance, regular meetings, and audited financial reporting.

Learner Focused

Every decision is made with the best interests of the learners in mind.

Our Vision

To be the leading scholar transport association in the region — recognised for safety, accountability, and excellence in service delivery to learners and their families.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable scholar transport by supporting operators, enforcing compliance standards, and fostering a culture of community responsibility across all routes.

Our Journey

2018
Founded

Zimeleni was established by 5 founding operators committed to improving scholar transport standards in Umlazi.

2020
Formal Registration

The association was formally registered as a Non-Profit Organisation and expanded to 3 schools.

2022
Growth & Compliance

Membership grew to 20 operators. A Code of Conduct was adopted and vehicle compliance inspections introduced.

2026
Today

847 learners transported daily across 12 schools and 24 routes. Digital management system introduced for full operational transparency.
